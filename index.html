Rapid decision simulation · ~5 minutes

Exercise Grey Heron

It's Monday, 08:47. Your systems are locked, your phones are ringing, and a journalist already knows more than you do. Six decisions stand between you and the evening news. You have 45 seconds for each one.

Situation

You are the senior leader on site at a mid-sized organisation. A cyber incident is unfolding in real time. Information will arrive as it does in real incidents: incomplete, from multiple directions, and faster than you'd like.

There are no trick questions — but there are consequences. Every choice is logged, timed, and debriefed at the end, exactly as it would be in a facilitated exercise.
