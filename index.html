Rapid decision simulation · ~5 minutes

Exercise Mercury

It's Thursday, 06:15. A red weather warning has just landed, the power is already flickering, and local radio wants answers before you've had coffee. Six decisions stand between you and the evening news. You have 45 seconds for each one.

Situation

You are the senior leader on duty at an organisation that delivers essential services to the public. A severe storm — high winds, a month's rain, widespread power cuts — arrives in hours. Information will arrive as it does in real incidents: incomplete, from multiple directions, and faster than you'd like.

There are no trick questions — but there are consequences. Every choice is logged, timed, and debriefed at the end, exactly as it would be in a facilitated exercise.
